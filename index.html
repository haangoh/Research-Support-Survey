Library Research Support Survey

This survey aims to gather feedback on the research support services offered to undergraduates, postgraduates, and academic staff. Your responses will help us understand your needs and expectations of library users and help us to improve and enhance Library services related to research support. For more information on Research Support Library Services currently provided, refer here.

Instructions:
The survey requires respondents to rate from 1 (lowest) to 5 (highest) for the two statements below:
1. Minimum level of research support services that you find acceptable
2. Level of research support services that you think or perceive the library currently provides
There are 18 questions which are mandatory. The Open Comment is optional. For items which are Not Related, please indicate "NA".
